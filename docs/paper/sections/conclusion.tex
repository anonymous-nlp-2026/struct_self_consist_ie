\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

NER exhibits oracle scaling ($c{\approx}0.5$) but on most datasets aggregation gains are ${<}1$\,pp; the diagnostic framework predicts the dataset-dependent optimal strategy, and entity construction yields conditional gains ($+0.32$--$1.40$\,pp) where the framework predicts favorable conditions.
The binding constraint is $\alpha{\to}0$ even when $\rho$ is substantial.
